Instructions

  • 1. New or Load a puzzle
  • 2. Drag each piece into the play area (dotted box)
  • 3. Use Rotate buttons for the selected piece
  • 4. Use Flip to mirror (useful for the parallelogram)
  • 5. Click Centre to centre all pieces in the play area
  • 6. The position of each piece becomes the target pose the player must match
  • 7. Click Save — puzzle appears in the game immediately
  • 💡 Same-shaped pieces (e.g. both large triangles) are interchangeable for the player
